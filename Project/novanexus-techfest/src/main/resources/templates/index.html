Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend the symposium?

+

We welcome professionals, researchers, students, and industry leaders globally. Dedicated tracks accommodate different experience levels.

Are accommodations provided?

+

We have partnered with leading hospitality providers in the area. Verified attendees receive a discount code for hotel bookings.

What is the refund policy?

+

Tickets are fully refundable until 30 days prior to the event. After that, they are transferable but non-refundable.

Is there virtual access?

+

Yes. Main keynote sessions and selected workshops will be broadcasted live on our proprietary streaming platform.

Can I volunteer at NovaNexus?

+

Absolutely. Volunteering applications open 3 months before the event. Volunteers receive free access to all sessions and exclusive merchandise.
